BLD & SAS War begins
 
 

Blood Clan declared war on the Sinners Among Saints (SAS).  On the 18th, BLD gated into Trinsic for battle.  Unfortunately, SAS was waiting and the BLD leaders got smacked hard leaving the gate.
 
After reequiping I found Sesmic Threat (I think Druidkin...) outside Vesper Bank.  He was told to move along.  He replied with a bolt and stealing my arrows.  After lots of running around and commotion, his crossbow of ass whooping finally drove me to monochromia.
Undaunted by death, BLD returned to Trinsic Bank and held it away from the cowardly SAS troops.  Further battles to come.
