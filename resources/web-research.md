# Sinners Among Saints (SAS) — Web Research

## Origins & Founding

- Founded **1996** during UO **Closed Beta** on the **Pacific shard** by **Rivan** (Alex Ronalds) and **Aries**
- Original name: **Sosarian Armed Services** — a mercenary guild named from Ultima lore
- Rivan and Aries played Warcraft, Red Alert, and Diablo together on **Kali** before UO beta
- Renamed to **Sinners Among Saints** circa 2000 by **NeXuS**

## Sonoma Era (1997-2004)

- Moved to **Sonoma shard** on launch day, **December 13, 1997**
- Immediately established as a **Player Killer (PK) guild**
- Signature: members appended "**of SAS**" to character names
- Key members: ChaoS, ArKaiNe, MoneyMaker, True Pimp, MasterMind, Psycho the Ripper, Drizzt Do-Urden, DragonMaster Dyn, Rainfall, SHADOW, NeXuS, MRGrimm
- **SHADOW's tower** in South Trinsic swamp became the guild HQ
- Also had a keep + tower complex east of Britain
- By mid-1998, membership exceeded **40+ players**

## Major Events & Wars

- **SAS vs Chaotic Horde** — First clan war, against Jeitarium's PKs in Trinsic jungles
- **SAS vs Lords of Evil (LoE)** — Started when Rivan used a gate-travel bug to break into LoE leader Charger's house and steal a Chaos Shield
- **Oasis 50th Fight Night** — Rivan won the Tournament of Champions (1999), first as contender then as "terrorist"
- **BLD vs SAS War** — Multi-month server-wide conflict: PKs vs Anti-PKs. SAS (Rivan, MRGrimm) vs Blood Clan (Grunk Half Orc, rukNar). Drew in allies from across the shard. No clear winner but mutual respect formed.
- **Rep Patch (Spring 1998)** — Origin's Stat Loss for Murderers changed the PK landscape

## Siege Perilous

- SAS recruited members for Siege Perilous Test server launch
- Ground gold fast, secured boats, farmed Deceit, got a house + guildstone
- Server was wiped — SAS lost everything
- Members who stayed formed **Disciples of the Moment (DTM)** led by **Meursalt** and the late **Orin Warrick**

## Free City of Oasis

- Player-run city on Sonoma founded by Jonas, Flaeme, Lady Rei, and Smitten w/Love
- Hosted weekly **Fight Night** on Sunday evenings (~1998-2000)
- Virtual population of ~100, boosted to 300 on Fight Nights
- Fighters battled in GM-made cages in organized tournaments
- The **50th Fight Night Tournament of Champions** invited back all 49 previous winners

## Beyond UO

- Expanded into: **Counter-Strike, Asheron's Call, World of Warcraft, Lineage**, and many more
- WoW: **Horde guild on Kil'Jaeden US** (PvP, PST timezone) — reroll guild
- Members span **North America, Europe, Asia, Australia**
- Members include: climatologists, lawyers, game developers, entrepreneurs

## Impact

- SAS's playstyle influenced how **Ultima Online's designers** crafted post-launch game mechanics
- Helped shape **modern MMORPG game design**
- One of the most feared guilds in UO history
